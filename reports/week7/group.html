There's steady progress being made! We're close to completing the MVP:

  • Added a variety of traps
  • Added a variety of items (different potions, e.g. healing, nausea-causing, etc., and wands) and inventory bar
  • Added a large part of the Dungeon Master player's functionality
  • Added torchlights (haha, get it? Mandatory ™ all lights reserved)
  • Added randomly generated mazes

  • Did a lot of work towards implementing animations in the game!

Some members in the group are stressed (given how much work is still to be done and given the time frame), but we're happy at how much progress we're making and are really excited to finish the MVP and start playing our game / adding more content and features!
