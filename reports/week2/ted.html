What are your specific goals for the next week?

    • Hard-coded structure of the map: The small map would help testing in early stages.
    • EventManager to manage incoming events: After meeting the goals above, we would need the event manager to control the movements of players and alterations of the gamestate, depending on which events that the client sends.
  • These components would set up the basic structure of the game.

What did you learn this week, if anything (and did you expect to learn it?)

  • I learned that making a game engine from scratch is much more complex than I had originally thought about. Every detail of implementing graphics, network, and etc. all have to come from scratch and we have to manually connect all the pieces. In a well-known game engine like unity, all these are done behind the scenes. This may be difficult, but I learned that a custom game engine gives more freedom in terms of how you want the game components to interact, which would be helpful for designing our game.

What is your individual morale (which might be different from the overall group morale)?
