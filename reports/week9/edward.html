If the week went differently than you had planned, what were the reasons?

  • There was a small issue where for some reason the DM would crash on reconnect. I was able to fix it by making sure to update the appropriate Session information after the DM player is assigned. Before there was no issue because the DM would be assigned as the first player on connect. However, now that the DM is actually being assigned AFTER everyone is ready to play, the appropriate Session information stored on connect isn’t updated. This means if the DM leaves & reconnects the stale stored Session information will assume it as a regular player and certain fields which shouldn’t be nullptr are nullptr and the game crashes. Thankfully it didn’t take me that long to fix.
